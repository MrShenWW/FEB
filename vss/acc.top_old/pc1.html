VSS profit method

  • Transaction fee

    When the user conducts digital currency trading, he/she needs to pay a certain percentage of the handling fee to the platform. In the later stage, different commission fees will be set according to operational needs and user historical transaction levels.

  • Cash withdrawal fee

    The digital currency is required to pay a certain amount of withdrawal fee for the platform.

  • Member service fee

    The user purchases the platform member service to obtain the transaction fee discount and value-added service, which serves as the operation means to lock the user.

  • Credit card fee

    The platform will launch some high-quality digital currency as the transaction target from time to time, and charge a certain amount of the foreign currency fee.

  • Leverage fee

    The platform will go online with the futures trading function, support leveraged transactions, and charge users a certain percentage of fees.
